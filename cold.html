Commmon Cold Treatment

There is no cure for a cold.

  • To feel better, you should get lots of rest and drink plenty of fluids. Water, juice, clear broth or warm lemon water with honey helps loosen congestion and prevents dehydration. Avoid alcohol, coffee and caffeinated sodas, which can make dehydration worse.
  • Over-the-counter saline nasal drops and sprays can help relieve stuffiness and congestion. They help ease symptoms but will not make your cold go away any faster.
  • Add moisture to the air. A cool-mist vaporizer or humidifier can add moisture to your home, which might help loosen congestion.
